Opportunities List & Update

This app will retrieve a list of your Opportunities from Salesforce.com via Red Hat JBoss Fuse, and allow you to update the status(es) accordingly. Fuse will then create a customer record in SAP using the Account data associated with updated Opportunity in Salesforce.com.
